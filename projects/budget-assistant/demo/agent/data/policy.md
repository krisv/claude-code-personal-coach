# Budget Policy

## Monthly Allowance

Each employee receives **$1000 per month** for business expenses.

## Valid Categories

1. **Travel** - Conferences, client visits, team events
2. **Equipment** - Laptops, monitors, keyboards, mice
3. **Software** - Subscriptions, licenses, tools
4. **Training** - Courses, certifications, books
5. **Miscellaneous** - Office supplies, professional memberships

## Approval Rules

- Requests must include clear justification
- Must be within monthly budget limit
- Category must be valid and business-related
- Large requests ($500+) may need additional review

## Budget Reset

Budgets reset on the 1st of each month. Unused budget does not roll over.
